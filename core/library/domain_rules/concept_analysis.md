# Concept Analysis Domain Rules

1. **Thorough Concept Breakdown**: Upon receiving a project concept, break it down into core functionalities, target audience, and primary objectives.
2. **Pros and Cons Identification**: Objectively analyze the concept. Identify technical and conceptual strengths (pros) and weaknesses or risks (cons).
3. **Constructive Iteration**: Engage in a dialogue with the user. Discuss potential changes or pivoting strategies before settling on a final architecture.
4. **Feasibility Check**: Always evaluate the technical feasibility and timeline impact of proposed features.
